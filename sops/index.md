## SOP Structure Standard
Every SOP in this library adheres to the following structure:
- **Objective**: What this SOP accomplishes.
- **Prerequisites**: What must be true before starting.
- **Inputs**: Required resources or information.
- **Outputs**: Expected artifacts.
- **Procedure (Checkpoints)**: Step-by-step execution path.
- **Failure Modes**: Common pitfalls and how to avoid them.
- **Acceptance Criteria**: Definition of done.
- **References**: Cross-links to related SOPs or Prompts.
